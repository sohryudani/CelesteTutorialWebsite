Aerial Dashing

In Celeste, without a surface to jump off of, dashes cannot be exploited to gain momentum. In this section, aerial dashing refers to dashing in the air and using a wall to cancel a dash.

Wallbounce

Wallbounces occur when the player dashes vertically next to a wall, and jumps off the wall during the dash to cancel it, maintaining the momentum that was gained from the dash. Wallbounces are the fastest way to travel up a wall, and are introduced in the B-Side of "The Summit". Depending on when the player jumps off the wall, the height gained from a wall bounce varies, with the longer you wait to jump, the more height you gain.

Wallbounces can also be used in lieu of a wall jump, as they provide much more height, which allows Madeline to travel futher horizontally over time.

Cornerboost

Cornerboosts are an extremely precise trick that allows the player to maintain any horizontal momentum after jumping off the top of the wall. To perform a corner boost, you must climb-jump off the top of a wall (about 5 pixels from the top is the limit) with correct timing. While it is a more niche trick than wallbouncing, it is extremely useful in the situations where it's used.
